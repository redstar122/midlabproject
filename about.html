Our Story

Rooted in the heart of San Fernando, Pampanga.

A Local Tradition

Founded in 2024, La Figlia Caffé started as a small dream to bring authentic, high-quality coffee and Kapampangan dishes to the local community. What started as a humble operation has grown into a beloved staple for families across Pampanga.

We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients and treating every customer like family. Our commitment to excellence and Kapampangan hospitality is what drives us every single day.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional food and coffee while fostering a sense of community and preserving our local heritage.
